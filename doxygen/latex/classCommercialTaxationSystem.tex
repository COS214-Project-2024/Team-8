Represents the commercial taxation system that collects business tax and sales tax from commercial entities. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommercialTaxationSystem}{Commercial\+Taxation\+System}} class is responsible for calculating and collecting business tax and sales tax from commercial businesses.
