\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the finance department of a government that manages taxation and budget allocation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classFinanceDepartment}{Finance\+Department}} class is responsible for collecting taxes from residential and commercial entities, maintaining financial records, and allocating funds for various public services and infrastructure projects.
