Detailed Description

Represents the budget allocation system that manages the distribution of funds for various public services and infrastructure.

The BudgetAllocationSystem class is responsible for allocating available funds to different categories of public services and infrastructure projects, such as medical centers, schools, police stations, utilities, transportation, and recreational facilities.
